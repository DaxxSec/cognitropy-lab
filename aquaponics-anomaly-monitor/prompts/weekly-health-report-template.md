# Weekly Health Report Prompt Template

Use at the end of each week to generate a structured summary.

---

**Prompt:**

```
Generate a weekly health report for my aquaponics system based on the following log data.

System: [brief description]
Report period: [YYYY-MM-DD] to [YYYY-MM-DD]

DAILY READINGS (paste your log or CSV):
[Date] | pH | Temp | NH3 | NO2 | NO3 | DO | Notes
[Date] | pH | Temp | NH3 | NO2 | NO3 | DO | Notes
[...7 rows...]

EVENTS THIS WEEK:
- [Any fish additions, treatments, equipment issues, water changes, harvests]

FISH OBSERVATIONS:
- Feeding response: [excellent/good/reduced/none]
- Behavior: [normal/any concerning observations]
- Any deaths: [yes/no]

PLANT OBSERVATIONS:
- Growth rate: [normal/fast/slow]
- Appearance: [healthy/yellowing/nutrient deficiency signs]
- Harvest/planting: [if any]

EQUIPMENT:
- Pump/flow: [normal/any issues]
- Aeration: [normal/any issues]
- Temperature control: [normal/any issues]
```

Include: parameter trend analysis, nitrogen cycle health score, any recurring issues, maintenance actions for next week, and anything I should watch closely.
