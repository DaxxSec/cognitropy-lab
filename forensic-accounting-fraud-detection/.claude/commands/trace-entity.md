# /trace-entity — Corporate Structure & Beneficial Ownership Mapping

I'll help you map an entity's structure, ownership chain, and associated risk indicators.

## What to Provide
- Target entity name and jurisdiction (state/country)
- Any known principals (officers, directors, owners)
- Known addresses, EINs, phone numbers
- Context: why is this entity of interest?

## What I'll Do
1. **Structure walkthrough** — guide you through a systematic registry search sequence
2. **Ownership chain analysis** — identify parent companies, subsidiaries, and beneficial owners
3. **Red flag assessment**:
   - Opacity jurisdiction indicators (Delaware LLC, Wyoming, BVI, Cayman, etc.)
   - Registered agent clustering (formation agent vs. substantive business)
   - Address recycling across multiple entities
   - Principal cross-appearance in multiple entities
4. **Consistency analysis** — does financial footprint match operational profile?
5. **Sanctions/adverse media checklist** — OFAC SDN, public court records, news

## Data Sources I'll Reference
- OpenCorporates (public, free)
- SEC EDGAR (for public companies)
- OFAC SDN List
- State corporate registry portals
- ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer (for 990 filers)

## Output
- Text-based entity relationship map
- Risk-ranked finding summary
- Recommended next data requests

Tell me the entity name and what you're trying to determine.
