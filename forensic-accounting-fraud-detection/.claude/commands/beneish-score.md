# /beneish-score — Financial Statement Manipulation Screen

I'll compute the Beneish M-Score and supporting ratio analysis to assess earnings manipulation risk.

## What to Provide
Two consecutive years of financial data:

**Income Statement:**
- Net sales (both years)
- Cost of goods sold
- Gross profit
- SG&A expense
- Depreciation/amortization
- Net income

**Balance Sheet:**
- Total current assets
- Cash and equivalents
- Receivables (net)
- Inventory
- PP&E (net)
- Total assets
- Current portion of long-term debt
- Total long-term debt
- Total liabilities

**Cash Flow Statement:**
- Operating cash flow

## What I'll Compute
1. All 8 Beneish M-Score variables (DSRI, GMI, AQI, SGI, DEPI, SGAI, TATA, LVGI)
2. Composite M-Score with interpretation
3. Variable-by-variable red flag analysis
4. Supplementary ratios: DSO, DIO, accruals ratio, cash flow quality
5. Altman Z-Score (if market cap available)

## Output
- Scored results table with benchmark comparisons
- Narrative interpretation of key risk drivers
- Recommended follow-up investigation steps if score is elevated

Provide the financial figures and I'll run the full analysis.
