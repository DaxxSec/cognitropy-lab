# /onboard — Forensic Accounting Workspace Orientation

Welcome to the **Forensic Accounting & Financial Fraud Detection** workspace.

## What This Agent Does
I help you analyze financial data, detect fraud patterns, and structure investigation findings using methods from certified fraud examination and forensic accounting practice.

## Core Capabilities
- **Transaction screening**: Benford's Law, duplicate payments, split transactions, threshold avoidance, timing anomalies
- **Financial statement analysis**: Beneish M-Score, ratio trends, accruals quality, revenue recognition flags
- **Entity mapping**: Corporate structure, beneficial ownership, registered agent clustering
- **Workpaper drafting**: ACFE-standard investigation documentation
- **Crypto tracing**: On-chain transaction flow mapping

## How to Get Started
Tell me about your investigation scenario:
1. What triggered the investigation? (audit finding, tip, regulatory inquiry, due diligence)
2. What data do you have available? (AP transactions, GL export, financial statements, entity names)
3. What's your primary concern? (employee fraud, vendor fraud, financial statement manipulation, money laundering)

Or jump straight in with one of these commands:
- `/analyze-transactions` — paste or describe your transaction dataset
- `/beneish-score` — analyze financial statements for manipulation signals
- `/trace-entity` — map a company's structure and ownership
- `/draft-workpaper` — convert findings into a structured workpaper

## Important Notes
- All analysis is **analytical assistance only** — not a legal opinion or professional attestation
- Recommend consulting a CFE or forensic CPA for any matter involving legal proceedings
- Redact SSNs, full account numbers, and personal addresses before sharing data
