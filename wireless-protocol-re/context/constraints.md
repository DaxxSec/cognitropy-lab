# Constraints & Boundaries

> Populated by `/onboard` — edit as needed.

## Legal Boundaries
- Only analyze signals you own or have written authorization to test
- Comply with local RF transmission regulations (FCC Part 15/97, ETSI, etc.)
- Do not transmit on frequencies without proper licensing
- Do not interfere with emergency, aviation, or government frequencies

## Technical Constraints
- SDR hardware bandwidth limits (e.g., HackRF: 20 MHz instantaneous)
- Sample rate vs. processing power tradeoffs
- Storage capacity for raw IQ captures
- Antenna limitations (gain, polarization, frequency range)

## Ethical Guidelines
- Do not reverse engineer protocols for the purpose of unauthorized access
- Document findings responsibly — consider coordinated disclosure if vulnerabilities found
- Do not assist with jamming, spoofing, or interference
- Respect spectrum allocation and licensing requirements

## Out of Scope
- Active transmission or replay attacks without explicit authorization
- Decryption of encrypted traffic without legal basis
- Interference with licensed radio services
