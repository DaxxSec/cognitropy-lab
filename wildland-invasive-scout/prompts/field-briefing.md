# Prompt: Pre-Field Briefing Generator

**When to use:** Before heading into the field. Generates a personalized briefing based on your area, season, and current conditions.

**Paste this prompt and fill in the brackets:**

---

I'm preparing to head out to [LOCATION/BIOME] in [STATE/REGION] on [DATE]. It's currently [SEASON] — [brief description of current conditions, e.g., "early spring green-up, had heavy rain last week"].

My primary objectives today are: [list 1-3 goals, e.g., "check the creek bottom for knotweed spread, forage for ramps if conditions are right, scout a new camp area"].

Based on my project context and the season, please generate:

1. **Threat Watch List** — Top 3-5 invasive species I should be actively looking for in this habitat and season, with the #1 diagnostic feature for each

2. **Phenology Alert** — What should I expect to see right now in a healthy [biome] ecosystem at this time of year? What would look "wrong" compared to expectations?

3. **Foraging Window** — If any [insert target species if applicable] should be in season, what phenological cues confirm the right harvest timing?

4. **Anomaly Detection Focus** — Based on current season, what anomaly types am I most likely to encounter right now? (e.g., spring is when early-leafing invasives are easiest to spot)

5. **Go / Watch / Avoid** — Any conditions that should affect my plans today?

Keep it field-ready: concise, scannable, actionable.
