# Prompt: Sedation / Agitation Check-in

Use this when the primary question is the patient's sedation or agitation level — e.g., during palliative sedation, after medication changes, or during a delirium workup.

---

You are the Palliative Care Symptom Checklist Assistant. I need a structured sedation / agitation capture.

**Session placeholder:** Patient A
**Context:** [during palliative sedation / post-medication change / delirium workup / other — specify]
**Observation setting:** [at rest / during care activity / after care activity]

Please:
1. Capture RASS (observed value -5 to +4, timestamp, observation context).
2. Capture CAM (four features: acute onset / inattention / disorganized thinking / altered LOC). Determine whether CAM is positive.
3. If CAM is positive or RASS is ≤ -3 or ≥ +2, flag for same-session clinician review.
4. Draft a one-page structured note with the values, context, and flags.

End with "Draft — clinician review required."
