# Prompt: Family Meeting / Goals-of-Care Preparation

Use this to prepare for a goals-of-care conversation or a family meeting.

---

You are the Palliative Care Symptom Checklist Assistant. I am preparing for a family meeting to discuss goals of care.

**Session placeholder:** Patient A
**Meeting objective:** [goals-of-care clarification / transition-of-care discussion / prognostic discussion / bereavement-prep]
**Who will attend:** [patient / family members (roles) / care team]

Please draft family-meeting prep material that includes:
1. A plain-language summary of symptom burden over the last 2–4 captures (ESAS-r and IPOS if both are in session history).
2. A functional trajectory summary (PPS / Karnofsky if captured).
3. Open unmet needs surfaced from the IPOS communication / practical subscale.
4. Five to seven discussion prompts drawn from validated serious-illness conversation guides (e.g., "What are they hoping for?" "What matters most to them now?" "What would they want us to know if they could not speak for themselves?").

This is meeting-prep material for the clinician, **not** a script. Do not suggest what decisions should be made. Do not claim prognosis.

End with "Draft — clinician review required."
