# Active Form Plans

This is the working backlog. Each entry is a candidate for `/form-sim`. Complete entries get archived into `planning/archive/<YYYY-MM>/`.

## Status Legend
- **`pending /form-sim`** — described, not yet simulated
- **`sim green`** — passed sim and scenario test
- **`sim yellow`** — passed sim with caveats; risk accepted (link to batch record where accepted)
- **`sim red`** — sim refused; needs rework
- **`scheduled <date>`** — has a session planned
- **`completed`** — produced; pieces logged
- **`archived`** — moved out of active backlog
