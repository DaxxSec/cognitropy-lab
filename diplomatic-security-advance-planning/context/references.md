## Vocabulary Disambiguation

- **"Tier"** — threat tier (T1-T4); different from "tier" as used in tradecraft (operational tier).
- **"Detail"** — protective detail (team accompanying protectee); different from "detail" as used in chronology.
- **"Advance"** — pre-trip planning team OR the act of pre-trip planning. Context-dependent.
- **"CAT"** — Counter-Assault Team (motorcade); not to be confused with civilian "CAT" (Critical Assistance Team) terminology used in some practices.
- **"Hard room" / "safe room"** — designated safe area at venue; not synonymous with hotel-suite secure-room.
- **"Sustained"** — actions beyond 5 minutes in contingency activation; not synonymous with "long-term."
