## Why This Workspace

Most signal retiming projects optimize control delay or LOS and treat air-quality co-benefits as a one-paragraph aside in the final report. That misses both the engineering insight (cycle/split choices that minimize delay can simultaneously increase emissions when stops-per-vehicle climbs) and the funding leverage (CMAQ, TCM eligibility, SIP credit). This workspace pairs the canonical HCM 7 / Webster / Akcelik capacity machinery with EPA MOVES4 and the CMEM modal emission model so that every cycle, split, offset, and coordination plan is generated on the delay × emissions × fuel × noise Pareto frontier and packaged with NEPA-grade environmental documentation.
