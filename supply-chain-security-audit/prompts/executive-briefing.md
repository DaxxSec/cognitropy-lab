# Executive Briefing Generator

Use this prompt to produce a non-technical summary of supply chain audit findings for leadership.

## Prompt

Based on our recent supply chain audit findings, please generate an executive briefing that:

1. Summarizes overall supply chain risk posture in business terms
2. Highlights the top 3-5 risks by business impact (not just CVSS score)
3. Explains what "comfort measures" are in place and what they protect against
4. Provides a clear remediation roadmap with resource estimates
5. Includes comparison to industry benchmarks where possible
6. Recommends investment priorities for supply chain security

Keep it to 1-2 pages. Avoid jargon — this is for C-suite and board consumption. Use the triage severity scale but explain it in plain language (e.g., "critical — requires immediate action" rather than "CVSS 9.8").
