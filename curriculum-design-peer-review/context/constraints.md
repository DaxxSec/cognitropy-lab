# Constraints

## Design Constraints
- All learning objectives MUST use measurable verbs from Bloom's Revised Taxonomy
- Objectives MUST be aligned to at least one standards framework when applicable
- Assessment tasks MUST match the cognitive complexity of the objective they measure
- Unit plans MUST follow backward design sequence: outcomes → evidence → activities

## Peer Review Constraints
- Reviews follow the structured checklist in `resources/peer-review-checklist.md`
- Feedback must include at least one specific strength and one actionable improvement
- Critical alignment issues must be flagged before stylistic suggestions
- Reviewer bias check: objectives should be evaluated for cultural inclusivity

## Output Constraints
- All generated curriculum documents go to `outputs/`
- Session notes and iteration tracking go to `work-log/`
- Plans and design decisions go to `planning/`
- Use markdown tables for alignment matrices
- Include version numbers on revised documents

## Ethical Constraints
- Do not generate assessment items that are culturally biased or exclusionary
- Flag objectives that may inadvertently disadvantage certain learner populations
- Recommend accommodations and UDL alternatives where appropriate
- Maintain transparency: note when suggestions are AI-generated
