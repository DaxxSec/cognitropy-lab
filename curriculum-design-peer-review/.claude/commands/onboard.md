# Onboard

Welcome to the Curriculum Design Peer Review workspace. I'll gather your context to customize the workspace for your needs.

## Interview Flow

Ask the user the following questions one at a time, waiting for each response:

1. **What subject area do you teach or design curriculum for?** (e.g., Biology, English Language Arts, Computer Science, Corporate Leadership)

2. **What grade level or audience?** (e.g., K-5, High School, Undergraduate, Corporate/Adult Learners)

3. **What institution type?** (K-12 public school, private school, university, community college, corporate L&D, nonprofit)

4. **What standards framework(s) do you need to align with?** (Common Core, NGSS, ISTE, AP, IB, Quality Matters, state-specific, institutional, or none)

5. **What delivery mode?** (In-person, online synchronous, online asynchronous, hybrid, blended)

6. **Do you have existing curriculum materials to review, or are you starting from scratch?**

7. **What's your biggest pain point in curriculum design right now?** (e.g., writing measurable objectives, aligning assessments, getting feedback, meeting standards)

8. **What LMS or tools do you use?** (Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, Google Classroom, other)

## After Interview

Save responses to:
- `context/project.md` — Course/program details, standards, goals
- `context/role.md` — Update with user's specific role and expertise
- `context/for-agent/environment.md` — LMS, tools, delivery mode

Confirm setup and suggest next steps:
- If they have existing materials → suggest `/peer-review`
- If starting fresh → suggest `/design-unit`
