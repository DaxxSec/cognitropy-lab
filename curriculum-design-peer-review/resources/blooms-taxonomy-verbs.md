# Bloom's Revised Taxonomy — Verb Reference

Quick-reference for writing measurable learning objectives.

## Level 1: Remember
**Definition:** Retrieve relevant knowledge from long-term memory
**Verbs:** define, duplicate, identify, label, list, match, memorize, name, order, outline, recall, recognize, repeat, reproduce, select, state

## Level 2: Understand
**Definition:** Construct meaning from instructional messages
**Verbs:** classify, compare, contrast, demonstrate, describe, discuss, distinguish, estimate, exemplify, explain, extend, illustrate, infer, interpret, locate, paraphrase, predict, report, restate, summarize, translate

## Level 3: Apply
**Definition:** Carry out or use a procedure in a given situation
**Verbs:** apply, calculate, carry out, choose, compute, construct, demonstrate, dramatize, employ, execute, illustrate, implement, interpret, modify, operate, practice, schedule, sketch, solve, use, write

## Level 4: Analyze
**Definition:** Break material into parts and determine relationships
**Verbs:** analyze, break down, categorize, characterize, classify, compare, contrast, debate, deconstruct, diagram, differentiate, discriminate, distinguish, examine, experiment, inspect, investigate, organize, question, relate, research, test

## Level 5: Evaluate
**Definition:** Make judgments based on criteria and standards
**Verbs:** appraise, argue, assess, choose, conclude, convince, critique, debate, decide, defend, determine, discriminate, evaluate, grade, interpret, judge, justify, measure, monitor, predict, prioritize, prove, rank, rate, recommend, select, support, value

## Level 6: Create
**Definition:** Put elements together to form a novel, coherent whole
**Verbs:** adapt, build, change, combine, compile, compose, construct, create, design, develop, devise, elaborate, extend, formulate, generate, hypothesize, integrate, invent, make, modify, organize, originate, plan, produce, propose, rearrange, reconstruct, revise, rewrite, synthesize, transform, write

## Verbs to AVOID (not measurable)
appreciate, be aware of, become familiar with, comprehend, cover, discover, enjoy, explore, gain knowledge of, grasp, know, learn, realize, study, think about, understand

These verbs describe internal states that cannot be directly observed or measured. Replace with specific Bloom's verbs.
