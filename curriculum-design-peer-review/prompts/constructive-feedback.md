# Constructive Feedback Template

Provide structured peer review feedback on a curriculum document.

## Prompt

Review the following curriculum material and provide feedback using this structure:

### Strengths (What's Working Well)
Identify 2-3 specific strengths. Quote the relevant section and explain why it's effective, citing the instructional design principle it exemplifies.

### Critical Issues (Must Address)
For each critical issue:
- **Issue:** [Specific problem, with quote]
- **Why it matters:** [Which principle or framework is violated]
- **Suggested revision:** [Concrete before/after example]

### Important Recommendations (Should Address)
For each recommendation:
- **Observation:** [What you noticed]
- **Rationale:** [Why this matters for learning]
- **Suggestion:** [How to improve]

### Enhancement Ideas (Could Address)
Brief bullet list of polish items or stretch goals.

### Overall Assessment
One paragraph summarizing the document's readiness and suggested next steps.
