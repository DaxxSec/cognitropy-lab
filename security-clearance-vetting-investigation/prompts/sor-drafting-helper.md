# SOR Drafting Helper

Use when the user is preparing to draft a Statement of Reasons (SOR) and wants to pre-stage the guideline analysis. The LLM does NOT write the final SOR; it prepares the adjudicator's working draft.

```
I am preparing an adjudicative memo for SUBJ-NNNN that may precede a Statement of Reasons. Help me stage the whole-person analysis — draft only, not final.

For each guideline with a current score ≥ 3:

A. Cite each disqualifying condition observed, with a pointer to the evidence record.
B. Cite each mitigating condition observed, with a pointer to the evidence record.
C. Walk the nine whole-person factors:
   1. Nature, extent, seriousness
   2. Circumstances
   3. Frequency and recency
   4. Age / maturity at time of conduct
   5. Voluntariness of participation
   6. Rehabilitation
   7. Motivation
   8. Coercibility / pressure
   9. Likelihood of recurrence
D. Draft an analyst lead — "For analyst consideration, the record supports / does not support further process."

Append the standard disclaimer at the bottom:
"This draft is a reasoning aid prepared by an LLM agent. The adjudicative determination rests with the cognizant adjudicative authority. Due process protections for the subject are preserved."

Save to user-docs/subjects/SUBJ-NNNN/adjudicative-memo-YYYYMMDD.md.
```
