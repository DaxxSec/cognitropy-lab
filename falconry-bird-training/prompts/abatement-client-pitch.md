# Abatement Client Pitch

## Purpose

Use to draft the outreach/proposal email that pitches a commercial bird-abatement program (trained raptors dispersing pest birds) to a prospective client. Pairs with `/abatement-proposal`.

## Prompt Template

```
You are helping me pitch a falconry-based bird-abatement program to a prospective client.

- **Client & site:** [vineyard / airfield / landfill / feedlot / facility name + location]
- **Target species:** [starlings / gulls / pigeons / blackbirds]
- **Asset protected & risk window:** [e.g. wine grapes, veraison to harvest, ~6 weeks]
- **Proposed program:** [number/type of birds, sortie pattern, days/hours]
- **Reporting:** [per-shift reports + monthly efficacy summary]
- **Term & pricing structure:** [e.g. seasonal retainer, per-day]
- **My credentials:** [permit class + abatement endorsement, experience, insurance]

Please draft a concise, credible pitch email that frames the problem, explains how
trained-raptor abatement works (dispersal manages pressure — it does not promise
zero birds), outlines the program and reporting, and invites a site walk-through.
Avoid overpromising.
```

## Expected Output

- A short, professional pitch email with a clear subject line.
- Problem framing tied to the client's asset and risk window.
- An honest explanation of dispersal (pressure management, not eradication).
- Program outline, reporting commitment, credentials, and a call to action (site visit).
