## Status Definitions
- **Active** - propagating in recent tests OR associated with natural activity in last 14 days.
- **Quiet** - no propagation in last 7-14 days across representative sampling, no natural activity.
- **Healing Candidate** - quiet for 14+ days, consolidation evidence (density / temperature / test character shift).
- **Healed** - requires human forecaster sign-off. Document evidence trail.
