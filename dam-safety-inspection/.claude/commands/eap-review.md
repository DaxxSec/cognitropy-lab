# EAP Review

Review a Dam Emergency Action Plan for completeness and adequacy.

## Usage
Provide the EAP document or describe its contents. I will check for:

## Required Components (per FEMA 64)
- Notification flowchart with current contact information
- Responsible personnel with alternates identified
- Dam and area description
- Failure/flood scenarios (both sunny-day and flood-induced)
- Inundation maps for each failure scenario
- Evacuation zones and routes
- Emergency detection, evaluation, and notification procedures
- Coordination with emergency management agencies
- Plan maintenance and update schedule

## Review Process
1. Component completeness check against FEMA 64 requirements
2. Scenario coverage assessment (sunny-day piping, overtopping, seismic)
3. Notification timing adequacy review
4. Contact information currency flags
5. Coordination gap identification
6. Prioritized improvement recommendations

## Output
A gap analysis report with specific recommendations for EAP improvement, prioritized by safety significance.
