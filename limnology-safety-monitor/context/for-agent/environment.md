# Environment & Equipment Reference

## Standard Field Equipment

### Water Sampling
- **Van Dorn bottle** — discrete depth sampling (horizontal or vertical)
- **Kemmerer sampler** — point-source depth sampling
- **Integrated tube sampler** — epilimnion composite sampling
- **Secchi disk** — transparency / light penetration measurement
- **Depth sounder / bathymetric transducer** — bottom profiling
- **Sediment grab (Ekman or Ponar)** — benthic sampling
- **Plankton net (63μm or 80μm mesh)** — zooplankton and phytoplankton tows

### In-Situ Instruments
- **Multi-parameter sonde** (YSI, Hydrolab, In-Situ) — DO, temp, pH, conductivity, turbidity, chlorophyll-a
- **Handheld pH meter** — backup/field verification
- **Handheld conductivity meter** — backup/field verification
- **Portable turbidimeter** — NTU field measurements

### Safety Equipment
- **Type III or V PFD** — required for all personnel within 3m of water
- **Throw bag (20m rope)** — water rescue
- **First aid kit (waterproof)** — field medical
- **Emergency whistle + signal mirror** — communication
- **Hypothermia wrap / space blanket** — cold water response
- **Dry bag** — electronics and documents protection
- **VHF marine radio** — communication on large water bodies
- **Personal locator beacon (PLB)** — remote site operations
- **Sun protection** — broad-spectrum SPF 50+, wide-brim hat, UV-blocking sunglasses

### Watercraft
- **Flat-bottom jon boat (14-16 ft)** — shallow lake/wetland access
- **V-hull aluminum (16-20 ft)** — open water, larger lakes
- **Canoe/kayak** — small streams, low-impact access
- **Pontoon platform** — stable instrument deployment

## Software & Data Tools

- **Water quality databases:** WQX (EPA), STORET, state-specific LIMS
- **GIS:** QGIS, ArcGIS for site mapping and watershed delineation
- **Data analysis:** R (with `lakemorpho`, `rLakeAnalyzer`, `wql` packages), Python (`pandas`, `scipy`)
- **Modeling:** CE-QUAL-W2, LAKE2K, SWAT for watershed modeling
- **Field data capture:** Survey123, KoboToolbox, or ruggedized tablet with custom forms

## Calibration Standards

- pH: 4.00, 7.00, 10.00 buffers (NIST-traceable)
- Conductivity: KCl standard appropriate to expected range
- Dissolved oxygen: air-saturated water or Winkler titration verification
- Turbidity: formazin or StablCal standards (0, 20, 100, 800 NTU)
