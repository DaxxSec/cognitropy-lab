# Starter Prompts — Railway Signaling Systems

Use these prompts to get the most out of Signal Mentor. Copy, adapt, and use them to kick off focused learning sessions.

---

## Onboarding & Setup

```
I'm a new Year 2 apprentice with [company name]. I have PTS and I've just finished my basic electrical training. I need help preparing for my first track circuit assessment next month. Can you set me up?
```

```
I've been a railway technician for 8 years but I've always worked on track. I'm moving into signaling and need to understand the basics — where do I start?
```

---

## Concept Explanation

```
/explain track circuits — I'm at Level 2 and my trainer keeps mentioning 'shunt resistance'. I don't fully get why it matters.
```

```
/explain approach locking — I understand route locking but I can't quite picture why approach locking is needed separately.
```

```
/explain ETCS Level 2 from first principles. I know conventional signaling well (Level 3 competent) but ETCS is new to me.
```

---

## Fault-Finding Practice

```
I'm practicing for my NVQ assessment. Can you give me a realistic track circuit fault scenario and talk me through how you'd expect me to diagnose it step by step?
```

```
Scenario: I arrive at a relay interlocking signal box and Signal XY1 is showing red when the section ahead is clear. No recent maintenance. Walk me through the fault-finding approach.
```

---

## Assessment Prep

```
/quiz L3 — I have my NVQ Unit 4 assessment next week on track circuit maintenance. Give me 5 questions at competent level.
```

```
I'm preparing for my IRSE Technician Licence interview. What topics are typically covered and what depth of knowledge is expected?
```

---

## Incident Analysis

```
/incident Ladbroke Grove 1999 — I want to understand the full signaling chain of events, not just the AWS acknowledgement part.
```

```
/incident — Hypothetical: A signal technician resets an axle counter without a full section walk because the lineman says 'it looks clear'. What could go wrong?
```

---

## Progress Review

```
/progress — I passed my axle counter reset assessment last week. Can you update my profile and tell me what I should work on next?
```

```
I've been working on this workspace for a few weeks. Give me an honest assessment of where you think my gaps are based on our sessions.
```
